# Weather Information

## How Weather Works
Weather is the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time. It involves temperature, precipitation, wind, and other atmospheric conditions.

## Measuring Temperature
Temperature is measured in degrees Celsius (°C) or Fahrenheit (°F). The Celsius scale is based on the freezing point (0°C) and boiling point (100°C) of water.

## Weather APIs
Modern weather applications use APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) to fetch real-time weather data. Popular weather APIs include:
- Open-Meteo: Free, open-source weather API
- OpenWeatherMap: Popular commercial weather service
- WeatherAPI: Another commercial option

## Climate
Climate refers to the average weather conditions over a longer period (typically 30 years). Different regions have different climate types:
- Tropical: Hot and humid year-round
- Desert: Very dry with extreme temperatures
- Temperate: Four distinct seasons
- Polar: Very cold, ice and snow
